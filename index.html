Your Hormonal Rhythm & Self-Care Architecture

1. Menstrual Phase

Energy Level: Reflective & low. Focus on gentle recovery, stretching, warmth, and eating iron-rich whole foods.

2. Follicular Phase

Energy Level: Rising creativity & physical stamina. Ideal time to begin new health regimes, organize projects, and increase high-intensity workouts.

3. Ovulatory Phase

Energy Level: Peak magnetic power, confidence, and high social vitality. Your metabolic rate shifts; prioritize raw greens, fiber, and community interaction.

4. Luteal Phase

Energy Level: Turning inward. Progesterone rises. Scale back high-stress cardio, choose calming mindfulness options, and add complex carbs to stabilize mood drops.
